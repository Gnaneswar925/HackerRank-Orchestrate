PROMPT - 6 


Implement decision_agent.py.

Inputs:

* ClaimExtraction
* VisionAnalysis
* EvidenceResult
* RiskAssessment

Decision hierarchy:

1. Images are primary source of truth.
2. User history cannot override clear image evidence.
3. Evidence requirements must be considered.

Output:
FinalDecision schema.

Rules:

SUPPORTED:

* claim matches visible damage
* evidence sufficient

CONTRADICTED:

* relevant object part visible
* claimed damage absent

NOT_ENOUGH_INFORMATION:

* insufficient evidence
* poor image quality
* wrong angle
* object not visible

Generate:

* deterministic decision logic
* detailed justifications
* supporting image IDs

Generate production-ready code.
